Final Synthesis

1) The divide is multi-causal

Cost, infrastructure, devices, skills, age, education, and systemic inequity interact together.

2) One policy is never enough

Effective action blends affordability support, infrastructure investment, and digital skills training.

3) Place-based strategies matter

Data-driven local planning is essential because barriers differ across communities.
